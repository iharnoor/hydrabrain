04

Posting sequence

  1. Pre-flight. Open / and /blog, confirm they load. Capture Shots 1-5 at 2×. Run the terminal command live for Shot 4 (it's the most convincing image).
  2. Pick the lane. Thread (Option B) converts best for technical credibility; the single tweet (Option A) is the fast-share. You can post A, then quote-tweet it later with the thread.
  3. Compose the thread. Paste tweets 1→4. Attach the image cue shown on each. Add alt text from the table to every image.
  4. Keep the loss in. Do not edit out "gbrain wins 1-hop." It's what makes someone trust the 88-vs-77. Removing it is how this becomes the overclaim.
  5. Land the CTA. Final tweet links the blog. Pin the thread. Consider tags: #RAG #LLM #AgentMemory. Mention gbrain respectfully - a fair comparison, never a dunk.
  6. After it's up. Reply with Shot 6 (the per-hop nuance) for anyone who asks "where does gbrain win?" - pre-empts the obvious pushback.
